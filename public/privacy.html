Privacy Policy

AURORA — Global Weather
Effective date: July 16, 2026
Developer: Khaled Alfaifi

AURORA (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a free weather application available as a website and mobile app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is used when you use AURORA and why. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who we are

AURORA is developed by Khaled Alfaifi. Contact for privacy questions: khaledalfaifi09@gmail.com.

2. Information we use

Depending on how you use AURORA, the following may be processed:

  • Location (optional). If you allow location access or tap “Near me”, the App uses your device’s approximate or precise location to show local weather. Location is used to fetch forecasts and is not used to build advertising profiles.
  • City searches and saved places. City names and coordinates you search or save may be stored on your device (for example in local storage) so the App can remember your preferences.
  • App preferences. Settings such as temperature units (°C / °F) and wind units may be stored on your device.
  • Weather-related questions (Ask AURORA). If you use the AI assistant, the text of your question and a compact summary of the current forecast context are sent to our AI proxy and then to the language model provider so an answer can be generated.
  • Technical data. Standard technical information may be processed by hosting and API providers (for example IP address, request time, and basic device/browser type) to deliver the service, security, and rate limiting.

3. What we do not collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your real name, phone number, or payment card inside the free App.
  • We do not sell personal information to data brokers.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the App for personalized ads.

4. How information is used

  • To display weather forecasts, air quality, and related details for places you choose.
  • To answer optional AI questions about the forecast you are viewing.
  • To remember settings and saved cities on your device.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the App (including preventing abuse of the AI feature).

5. Third-party services

AURORA relies on third-party services to work:

  • Open-Meteo — weather, geocoding, and air quality data. When you view weather, requests are made with location coordinates or place search terms. See Open-Meteo terms.
  • xAI (Grok) via our AI proxy — if you use Ask AURORA, your question and limited weather context are processed to generate a reply. Content is handled according to the provider’s policies. See xAI legal.
  • Cloudflare Workers — may host the AI proxy that forwards Ask AURORA requests (rate limiting and delivery). See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — may host the website version of AURORA. See GitHub site policy.
  • Apple — if you download AURORA from the App Store or use platform services (notifications, analytics you enable at the system level), Apple’s privacy terms apply to those platform features.

These providers process data on their infrastructure under their own privacy terms. We only send what is needed for the feature you used.

6. Data stored on your device

Preferences, last location, and saved cities are typically stored locally on your device (for example using browser or app local storage). You can clear this data by clearing the website’s site data or deleting/reinstalling the app.

7. Children

AURORA is a general-audience weather app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children for marketing. If you believe a child has provided sensitive information through the AI chat, contact us and we will help address it as appropriate.

8. International processing

Weather and AI providers may process data on servers in different countries. By using AURORA, you understand that your requests may be handled outside your country of residence, subject to applicable laws and the providers’ safeguards.

9. Data retention

  • On-device settings remain until you clear them or uninstall the App.
  • AI requests are processed to generate a reply and for security/rate limiting; we do not operate a public profile of your chat history.
  • Provider logs may be retained for a limited time for security and operations.

10. Your choices

  • Location: deny location permission and search for a city manually instead.
  • AI: simply do not use Ask AURORA if you prefer not to send questions to the AI service.
  • Local data: clear site data / reinstall the app to remove saved cities and preferences.

11. Security

We use HTTPS where applicable and keep the AI API key on a server-side proxy (not embedded for users to copy). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; use the App with that understanding.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will change when we do. Continued use of AURORA after an update means you accept the revised policy.
